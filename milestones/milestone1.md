## Reflection

### 1. What went well during this unit?

We successfully identified that relying on ad-hoc communication across different platforms during a busy holiday weekend does not work for this group. Pinpointing this early allows us to pivot toward a more structured, centralized workflow moving forward.

### 2. What were some challenges your group faced in this unit?

It is difficult to coordinate many people since many people were busy for the holiday weekend, and some people did not like to use Slack and some people did not have Discord.

### 3. What additional support will you need in upcoming units as you continue to work on your final project?

Given the communication bottlenecks we experienced across different platforms, we could use guidance or a standardized team policy on centralizing our project communications and task assignments. Additionally, both our project management platform and branching strategy need to be formalized so we can establish clear rules managing user stories, pull requests, code reviews, and branch naming.
